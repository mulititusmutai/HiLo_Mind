# HiLo_Mind
> **Development of a Micromouse Robot based on DFS, A*, and Neural Network Machine Learning.**

## Vision Statement
To bring autonomous technology to the consumer market by bridging the gap between high-level capability and low-cost hardware.

## Core Goal
The primary objective of **HiLo_Mind** is to democratize autonomous technology through a "software-heavy, hardware-light" approach. By prioritizing algorithmic efficiency over expensive components, we aim to make sophisticated robotics accessible and sustainable.

---

## Strategic Targets

### 1. Energy Sustainability
* **Approach:** Implementing low power-consuming hardware paired with precision-engineered software. 
* **Impact:** By optimizing software, we reduce the dependency on high-power sensors and actuators. 
* **Efficiency:** High processing power is not a prerequisite for "low-level" sensing tasks. This philosophy results in a significant net power saving, proving that autonomous systems can be both lean and capable.

### 2. Technology Accessibility
* **Approach:** Shifting from high-fidelity, high-cost sensors to affordable, off-the-shelf hardware.
* **Impact:** Since high-quality algorithms cost no more to deploy than low-quality ones, superior software can extract maximum potential from inexpensive components. This drastically lowers the barrier to entry for developers and consumers alike.

### 3. Culture Shift
* **Approach:** Challenging the industry assumption that "good" autonomy requires massive datasets and exorbitant hardware budgets.
* **Impact:** HiLo_Mind serves as a proof of concept that intelligent software design can bridge the hardware gap, fostering a mindset of resourcefulness and engineering ingenuity.

---

## Path to Implementation: The Bigger Picture
This approach creates a scalable blueprint for technological independence. By focusing on sustainability and cost-effectiveness, countries like **Kenya** can move beyond importing solutions and begin building proprietary technology.

### Potential Applications:
* **Autonomous Factories:** Streamlined, energy-efficient production lines.
* **Reconnaissance Robotics:** Low-cost, expendable, yet highly capable scouts.
* **Assistive Mobility:** Affordable autonomous wheelchairs for increased independence.
* **Service Robots:** Practical automation for the service and hospitality industries.

HiLo_Mind intends to position its developers as frontiers in **energy sustainability, cost reduction, and global technology accessibility.**
